Ugly Tub has been the leader in Tub Refinishing in the Midwest for over 20 years

We don't just fix your tub, we fix it properly. We have built a family of loyal customers and dedicated employees. When you choose Ugly Tub you choose to go with over 20 years of history and practice, and proffesionals who have honed their craft through the last couple decades.

Work some bath-magic on your ugly bathroom

Founded in 1993, Bath Magic is in the business of reglazing bathroom and kitchen fixtures. We can make that old tub, tile, or sink look great again at a fraction of the cost of replacement. There is no need to remove them, because we can do the work right inside your home. With our service, you avoid the mess and high cost associated with replacement. Consider not only the cost of the new tub, but also the replacement of plumbing, fixtures, walls, and floors around the tub, which are always damaged during removal. Our finish is strong, durable, and easy to care for. No more scrubbing with harsh cleaners. Most jobs take just a few hours to complete. Appointments set Monday- Saturday.
